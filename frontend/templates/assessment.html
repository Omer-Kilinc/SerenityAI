Mental Wellbeing Self-Assessment

A simple tool to help you understand your current mental wellbeing

This brief assessment will help you gauge your current stress and anxiety levels. Please answer honestly - your responses are not stored or shared.

Rate each statement based on how you've felt over the past two weeks.

1. I've been feeling overwhelmed by my responsibilities.

2. I've had trouble relaxing or "turning off" my mind.

3. I've experienced physical symptoms of stress (headaches, muscle tension, etc.).

4. I've had difficulty sleeping due to worrying or anxiety.

5. I've found it difficult to focus on tasks or activities I enjoy.

Note: This assessment is not a diagnostic tool. It's designed to help you reflect on your mental wellbeing. If you're experiencing significant distress, please consult with a mental health professional.
